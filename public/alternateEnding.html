A Day in the Life: Part 4

If i decided to go out instead of staying in and doing work, then the night would go very differently. I would not want to stay in and do assignments when my friends are out having a good time. Every now and then, I do choose to go out instead of staying in. It is a lot of fun. Also a lot of work. Firstly, I have to see whether my friends are going just to a pub, or to a pub and then to a nightclub or someplace else after we hang in the pub.

Money is an issue when going out. Because I am a student, I am broke most of the time. most of my money goes into the bus and food, so finding money to go out is sometimes an issue but it has never stopped me from going out. I don't always need money to have a good time. But it does help.

Nights out are pretty ordinary whenever I decide to go out. Go to a pub. Have a few drinks with friends. Talk about what we've done this week. Talk about other things and then maybe go to a nightclub and have a nice time. Pretty typical.
