A Day in the Life: Part 3

At the end of the week I usually have to option of either going out with friends or staying home and doing assignments or study. In the past I'd choose to go out all the time but lately since the college work has been increasing in difficulty and quantity of work I have to do, I now choose to stay in and do college work instead of leaving it last minute.(copied to chapter 4)

The work I usually do is the work I refuse to do throughout the week because its either too hard, too long or there just isn't enough time throughout the week for me to do all of the work I am supposed to. It is exhausting but it has to be done. I don't just sit down and do all the work in one sitting. I take my time and not stress myself out by doing an assignment in one sitting. I tend to start it, take a break, come back to it in a while and do some more, and on and on. This is done until most of the assignment is done.(copied to chapter 4)

Once the majority of the assignment is done there isn't much left to do. I will finish the assignment in college next chance I get. All that is left to do is relax and regret not going out when I see how much fun my friends are having. But on the bright side, I'd have the majority of my work done so I can sleep easy knowing that that piece of work id off my mind.(copied to chapter 4)
